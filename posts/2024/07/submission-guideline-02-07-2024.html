Submission Guidelines for Fun of Physics Blog

We are thrilled to invite you to contribute articles to our platform, where we publish content on a diverse range of subjects including Literature, Science, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, History, Culture, and Computer Science. Your unique insights and perspectives are invaluable to us, and we look forward to sharing them with our readers.

Submission Requirements

  1. Originality: All submissions must be original work. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited. Articles should not be copied from any other sources, including books, websites, or other publications.
  2. Language: Articles can be written in either English or Bengali.
  3. Format: The typed digital format manuscript will be preferred.
  4. Visual Aids: If your article includes pictures, diagrams, or other visual aids, please provide them as separate files. Ensure that these visuals are of high quality and relevant to the content of your article.
  5. Permissions: If you use any third-party images, diagrams, or excerpts, ensure that you have the necessary permissions to use them. Proper credits must be given to the original creators.
  6. Content Quality: Ensure your article is well-researched, informative, and engaging. It should provide value to our readers and contribute positively to their understanding of the subject.
  7. Submission Details: Submit your article along with any accompanying visuals to physicsfunof@gmail.com. Include a brief author bio and any relevant contact information.
  8. Review Process: All submissions will undergo a review process. We reserve the right to edit your article for clarity, grammar, and length. Authors will be informed of any significant changes before publication.
  9. Acknowledgment: Upon acceptance, you will receive an acknowledgment email. If your article is not accepted, we will provide constructive feedback where possible.
  10. Publication: Once your article is approved, it will be scheduled for publication on our website. You will be informed of the publication date.

Additional Information

  • Deadlines: There are no strict deadlines for submission, but timely submissions are encouraged.
  • Revisions: Authors may be asked to make revisions based on feedback from our editorial team.

We are excited to read your submissions and share your knowledge with our audience. Thank you for contributing to the Fun of Physics blog!
